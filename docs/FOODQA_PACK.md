# Fieldgrade FoodQA pack

## Positioning

**Fieldgrade FoodQA** is the first flagship vertical pack for audit-ready provenance and evidence control in food, beverage, QA, and production teams.

## What it manages

- batch and event evidence
- deviation records
- CAPA records
- calibration certificates
- supplier COA and approval evidence
- SOP-linked notes and investigations
- exportable audit packs

## Ideal buyers

- QA managers
- technical managers
- site leads preparing for customer or certification audits
- consultants supporting food safety and compliance programs

## Core modules

- batch record intake
- deviation and incident record
- supplier evidence register
- calibration register
- investigation timeline
- audit-pack export

## Why FoodQA first

FoodQA is a grounded first vertical because the buyer pain is concrete, evidence-heavy, and repetitive. Teams already handle the evidence; Fieldgrade makes it sealed, reviewable, and exportable.

## Demo storyline

1. Import batch, calibration, and supplier evidence.
2. Attach a deviation note and review decision.
3. Build the investigation timeline.
4. Export the audit pack.

## Commercial message

FoodQA gives technical teams a fast governance layer for the evidence they already produce, without waiting for a full ERP or QMS replacement.
