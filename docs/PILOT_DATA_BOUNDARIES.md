# Pilot data boundaries

## What Fieldgrade is for

Fieldgrade helps teams capture evidence, seal provenance, review decisions, govern AI-assisted work, and export proof packs.

## What Fieldgrade does not certify

Fieldgrade does not certify compliance, replace qualified auditors, replace QA managers, or make regulator-approved decisions.

## AI handling boundary

AI-generated content is treated as review material, not automatic truth. Human review remains mandatory before operational, legal, or compliance decisions.

## Local-first expectation

Pilot users who need local-only handling should keep Fieldgrade and its storage volumes on controlled devices or hosts and avoid enabling external AI endpoints.

## Backups

Pilot users should back up runtime and artifact paths, keep those backups access-controlled, and separately manage retention or deletion of exported proof packs.

## Workspace deletion

Delete the local runtime and artifact paths or the corresponding Docker volumes, then verify that copied exports, screenshots, and backups have also been removed where required.
